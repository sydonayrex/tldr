# Agile Outcomes Process

## Outcome vs Output
- **Output**: "Shipped the export feature" (activity)
- **Outcome**: "40% of analysts use scheduled reports" (behavior change)

## Outcome Definition
Every work item must define:
1. **Current behavior**: What users do now (baseline metric)
2. **Target behavior**: What we want them to do (target metric)
3. **Leading indicators**: What we track weekly to predict outcome
4. **Kill criteria**: If indicator doesn't move by date X, stop investing

## Self-Correction
- Feature shipped but no behavior change? Kill or iterate
- Metric not moving? Reassess hypothesis
- Output-focused planning? Reframe as outcomes
