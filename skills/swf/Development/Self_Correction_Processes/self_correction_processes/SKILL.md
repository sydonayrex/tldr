# Self-Correction Process

## Principles
1. **Blameless**: Focus on system, not person
2. **Fast**: Correct within hours, not weeks
3. **Systemic**: Fix the process, not just the symptom
4. **Transparent**: Share learnings with whole team

## Triggers
- Bug escapes to production
- SLO breach
- Missed commitment
- Team conflict
- Process failure

## Process
1. **Detect**: Monitoring, feedback, or team member raises concern
2. **Acknowledge**: Admit the issue without blame
3. **Analyze**: Root cause (5 Whys, fishbone)
4. **Fix**: Implement corrective action
5. **Verify**: Confirm fix works
6. **Share**: Document and communicate learning

## Self-Correction
- Pattern of same issue? Escalate to systemic fix
- Correction not working? Reassess root cause
- Team afraid to admit mistakes? Psychological safety issue
