# Trust & Accountability Process

## Principles
1. **Trust by default**: Assume competence and good intent
2. **Verify respectfully**: Check work without micromanaging
3. **Own your domain**: You are the expert, act like it
4. **Admit gaps**: "I don't know" is a strength, not weakness
5. **Give feedback early**: Don't let issues fester

## Accountability Mechanisms
- **Daily**: Standup honesty (blockers, misses)
- **Per slice**: Peer feedback (what went well, what to improve)
- **Per cycle**: Anonymous trust survey
- **Continuous**: Direct feedback between team members

## Feedback Framework (SBI)
- **Situation**: When/where
- **Behavior**: What happened (observable)
- **Impact**: Effect on you/team/work

## Self-Correction
- Trust declining? Facilitate team discussion
- Feedback not given? Model it, create safety
- Accountability avoided? Address directly, don't escalate prematurely
